Reality Manga

Reality Manga made by p was released in 2022.

The main character is an orphan named Blue. He is a 14 year old that just started High School. Blue doesn't get along with people easly. Some would say that he's an arrogant jerk, but it's really not like that. Blue is a lazy dude who has a BIG dream.

His dad is the strongest man ever and his mom is a strong woman from Reality 3. Blue is the one who killed his father when he was 4 years old and his mom died while giving birth to him. He doesn't remember much about his mom and he clearly doesn't know how strong his dad was.

At the time when the story starts there are wars on who will rule the Earth.

Watch Blue achive his mom's dream and conquer the whole Reality.
